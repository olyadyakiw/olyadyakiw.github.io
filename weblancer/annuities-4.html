More Information about Annuities
What exactly is an Annuity?
An annuity is an investment product that provides safe, tax-deferred growth for your retirement portfolio. Annuities are considered low-risk, and can provide guaranteed, monthly income when you retire. There are many types of annuities with varying features & benefits that can supplement retirement. These include: fixed, variable, and indexed. We strongly suggest you consult with a financial professional to help you understand the various types of annuities before you make a decision.
Is an Annuity Right for You?
Annuities are could be a good fit for anyone. Annuities are customizable and designed for people looking for guaranteed, monthly income when they retire. They are considered to be a safe, reliable form of income that the account owner can not outlive.

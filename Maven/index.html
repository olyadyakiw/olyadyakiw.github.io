On-demand, ongoing care that changes lives

Shayna was hoping for a perfect pregnancy, but a preeclampsia diagnosis put her at risk of complications.

Maven connected her with a high-risk OB-GYN to help navigate her preeclampsia, and a certified doula helped create a birth plan to ease Shayna’s anxiety.

Lindsey knew that she wanted a family, but she worried about how diabetes would impact her fertility.

Attending a live fertility education class and meeting with a nutritionist through Maven helped Lindsey take control of her reproductive health and her wellbeing.

The stress of trying to conceive after a miscarriage was taking a toll on Lina and Jay’s relationship, as were their worries about the cost of fertility treatments.

Maven helped them navigate their fertility benefit coverage, then connected them to a relationship coach and a community of members who knew what they were going through.

Luka was thrilled to be a dad of two, but once his wife began traveling for work again, bedtime for their newborn and toddler became a struggle.

Luka worked with an infant sleep coach to establish a bedtime routine that worked for both kids, and Maven Milk helped his wife ship her breast milk home whenever she traveled.
